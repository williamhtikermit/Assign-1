Project Idea

Overview

My project idea is to make primarily an end-to-end encryption messaging app that is available both online and offline but specifically targeted toward the people of Myanmar. I want this app to available on phones, laptops or tablets. But I want target smartphones first since every people in the country has one in their hands. Then I hope I can move on the rest devices.

Motivation

Recent events in my country, Myanmar led to have a spark in this idea. Since February, there is a coup and many internet services, including Wi-Fi, fibers or mobile data have been shut down. So not many people have access to news online or able to communicate other safely online. Therefore, I want to make a messaging app offline that can be communicated via Bluetooth and network for particular area. Additionally, citizens have no guarantee whether government can tap into their phone calls and SMS messages.

Description

< Although my project idea is primarily a messaging app, it will have wide range of features. Mainly, the app will be divided into online and offline. Since this app will be mainly targeted toward the people of Myanmar, the language setting will be in Burmese default. This is the best option since Burmese is the primary language in the country. However, users will be able to change to English if they ever wanted. Many more languages are going to be added in future updates. As for the offline aspect, users will be able to use Bluetooth or Mesh network created by the devices or sign in same network without needing Internet connection. When users open the app with Bluetooth it will create a Mesh network. For the online communication features, users must setup their account with their mobile numbers. Users will be allowed to sync with the contacts on their devices if the permission from the users is ever granted. If not, users are able to make and add separated contacts on this app. All the chats, phone call and video calls are end-to-end encrypted making a safe place for users to communicate. Group chats and group calls are also available in the app. With the permission of users, they can choose to import all the photos and videos or selected ones of their choice and will be able to share with other users. With users’ permission, they can set any timer for the messages or shared photos and videos to disappear. Finally, users will be also to sync their call history or messages with other devices, such as tablet or laptops.

Tools and Technology

As for the tools and technology, API and Software Development Kits are essential. Several teams will be needed which each specific role. For instance, while one of the team is working on coding another will be working on UI/UX. There will also be beta testers to fix bugs and crashes.

Required Skills

To make this app into reality, experienced programming skills are needed. Developers need to know more than a programming language. Depending on the platform the app is going to be released, language going to be used will be different. For instance, java or python will be mainly used for android or window application whereas Swift will be used for iOS and MacOS application.

Outcome

When this app is at the hand of people, it would be huge improvements and contribution to the people of third world country like Myanmar. This app will be essential and must have app on everyone’s devices since it will be a safe place for people to communicate with tones of features packed in one.
